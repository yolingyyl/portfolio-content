Giant Bicycle

Giant Bicycle is a global bicycle company that provides abundant services including bike rental, travel tours, competitions/courses, and bike transportation. However, since every business unit operates its service separately, it was hard to collect and reutilize the information. Therefore, our team’s job is to digitalize all the operations by providing a management system for admins and an official website for customers. This was the second highest revenue project my company ever had, and as a newbie, I was flattered to be trusted as the project owner.

  • Timeline: 10 months+
  • Respsonsibility: Project Owner — stakeholder interview, PRD (product requirement documents), wireframes, database review, timeline management
  • Team size: 7 people in dev team, 10+ stakeholders

Rental Perfect: finds your perfect spot

This is the final project for ccClub Python. Our team developed a tool that helps people find the best rental spot based on their salary, work location, and rental preferences. We used the data source from 591 Rental and Taipei Metro websites, calculated the input customizations, and listed the top 20 most cost-effective metro stations.

  • Timeline: 3 weeks (2021 June)
  • Respsonsibility: Web scrapping of all the rentals on 591 rental
  • Team Size: 4 people
  • Tools: Python (requests, beautifulsoup, selenium, pandas, etc)
